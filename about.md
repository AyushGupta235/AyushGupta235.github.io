I'm a Machine Learning Engineer working on agentic AI systems—building production-grade applications of LLMs that go beyond demos into real-world workflows. My work sits at the intersection of applied ML, system design, and product thinking, where models need to operate reliably under constraints, not just perform well in isolation.

I'm particularly drawn to the messy, interesting problems of deployment: how you orchestrate multiple components, manage latency and reliability trade-offs, and make AI actually useful at scale. Over the next few years, I want to deepen my technical expertise in ML before eventually moving closer to the product side of technology.

I graduated from IIT (BHU) Varanasi with a BTech and MTech in Mathematics. Before my current role, I interned across ML research (CWNU, South Korea), consulting (EY-Parthenon), product strategy, venture capital, and a prior AI startup—which gave me an unusually wide lens on how technology and business intersect.
